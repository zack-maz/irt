## Implications for Plan 04 (R-02 calibration)

Key observation: `low_score: 0`. `MIN_NOTABILITY_SCORE=15` is currently non-binding — every
admitted facility already clears the score floor. That means the current scoring model plus
the Priority-Country bypass is admitting nearly every named facility in the priority set,
which explains dams=4588. Tuning the floor alone will not move dams. Specific levers Plan 04
should consider:

- **Dams at 4588 (9× over target, biggest miss):** The priority-country bonus (`isPriorityCountry`)
  is likely admitting every named dam in the 29-country ME set without requiring notability beyond
  a name. Plan 04 should either (a) tighten D-06 so priority-country admission requires
  `hasCapacityData` OR notability score ≥ threshold, effectively removing pure-name + priority-country
  admission for dams, or (b) raise MIN_NOTABILITY_SCORE to 40+ to make the priority-country bypass
  conditional on real notability. Option (a) is cleaner — the D-06 formula already names the
  three alternatives explicitly.

- **Reservoirs at 892 (~2× over target):** `no_city: 244` is firing (the reservoir-only
  priority-or-named-with-city rule is working as designed). `not_notable: 12942` is pulling most
  of the weight on reservoirs already. The remaining over-admission is probably named reservoirs
  in priority countries whose only signal is the name itself. Plan 02's D-06 compound gate
  (hasName AND (isNotable OR isPriorityCountry OR hasCapacityData)) will land before Plan 04;
  that change alone may not cut reservoirs enough if `isPriorityCountry` is treated as
  sufficient. Plan 04 should expect to add a reservoir-specific secondary signal requirement.

- **Desalination at 63 (~2.5× over target):** Raw count is exactly 63 (100% admission). The
  plants include legitimate Gulf desalination facilities (Jubail, Ras al-Khair, Sorek) alongside
  smaller named plants. Plan 04 should inspect whether a capacity floor (e.g., `volume > 50000 m³/d`)
  trims this to the 13-25 band observed in prior refreshes without losing the headline plants.

- **`excluded_location: 2423`, `not_notable: 12942`, `no_name: 2965`** are all active gates —
  no rule is currently dead. The name requirement (D-05) lands cleanly on ~2965 raw elements.

- **Enrichment sparse:** `withCapacity: 149 / 5374 = 2.8%`, `withCity: 2158 / 5374 = 40.2%`,
  `withRiver: 145 / 5374 = 2.7%`. Capacity data is rare in OSM for this region; Plan 04 should
  not lean hard on `hasCapacityData` as a primary admission signal because it would fail open
  for non-priority-country facilities. It works as a supplement, not as the main gate.

needs to measure.
