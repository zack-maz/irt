## Intentional Asymmetries from WaterFiltersSection

Documented in the component's JSDoc and enforced by regression tests:

| Dimension                | Water panel                                                                | Sites panel (this plan)                                                          |
| ------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Rejection buckets        | 6 (excluded_location, not_notable, no_name, duplicate, low_score, no_city) | 4 (excluded_turkey, no_coords, no_type, duplicate)                               |
| Per-type rejection split | Yes (`Rejections by Type` block — one line per facilityType)               | **No** — sites adapter uses one combined Overpass query across 5 types           |
| Score histogram          | Yes (`Scores:` line at bottom)                                             | No (sites has no notability scoring per Plan 07 §"R-05 Observability Asymmetry") |
| Enrichment coverage      | Yes (`Enriched: cap=X city=Y river=Z`)                                     | No (sites has no enrichment pipeline)                                            |

All four omissions follow Plan 07's explicit guidance: _"Do NOT invent
synthetic buckets. Sites has a genuinely narrower rejection surface because
the adapter is simpler (no compound admission gate, no scoring, no
nearestCity requirement)."_
