## Desalination Audit Report

### Methodology
Queried Overpass API with `[bbox:15,30,42,70]` for `man_made=desalination_plant` and `water_works=desalination` tags, matching the server's `overpass-water.ts` adapter query. Cross-referenced results against known major Gulf desalination plants from RESEARCH.md.

### Key Findings

1. **Israel completely absent** -- No Israeli desalination plants in OSM with recognized tags. This is a significant gap given Israel's dependence on desalination (85%+ of domestic water).
2. **Kuwait severely underrepresented** -- None of Kuwait's 5 major plants found.
3. **Qatar completely absent** -- Neither Ras Abu Fontas nor Umm Al Houl present.
4. **Oman absent** -- Neither Barka nor Sur found.
5. **Saudi partially covered** -- Jubail 3A/3B and Shoaiba found, but Ras Al Khair (world's largest) missing.
6. **UAE partially covered** -- Taweelah and Jebel Ali present, Fujairah F2 missing.
7. **Jebel Ali over-granular** -- 40+ individual RO unit polygons instead of one plant-level feature. Our `["name"]` filter in the Overpass query filters these out (they're unnamed), so only the overall facility shows if named.

### Recommendation
Per user decision: **report-only, no manual data addition.** OSM coverage of desalination plants is weak for this region. If higher coverage is desired in the future, options include: (a) contributing to OSM, (b) maintaining a supplementary static dataset, (c) using IRENA or IDA (International Desalination Association) datasets.
