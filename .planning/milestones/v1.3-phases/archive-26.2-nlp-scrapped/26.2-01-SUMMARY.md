## Decisions Made

- Population threshold raised from 50k to 200k: the bbox+country filter at 50k produced 1027 cities (far above 250 target). 200k yields 303 cities across 21 countries with good conflict-zone coverage
- Added 6 regional countries beyond the 16 GDELT FIPS set (EG, SD, SO, DJ, ER, CY) for broader geolocation coverage
- Conflict actor names (Houthi, Hamas, Hezbollah, Taliban, IRGC, etc.) added to compromise lexicon since they aren't recognized as proper nouns by default
- Multi-word city names matched via direct substring fallback since compromise tokenizes hyphenated names (e.g., "Deir ez-Zor") into separate tokens
